Welcome To ICVMWT 2024

It gives us immense pleasure to invite you to join ICVMWT-2024 (International Conference on VLSI & Microwave and Wireless Technologies), which will be held in one of the most ancient and holy cities of India, Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, from May 17-18, 2024. The conference is organized by Madan Mohan Malviya University of Technology, Gorakhpur, with the aim of providing a platform for researchers, scientists, technocrats, academicians, and engineers to exchange innovative ideas and discuss new challenges in the field of Electronics Engineering. This conference will be a platform to focus on the core technological developments in emerging fields such as VLSI, RF microwave/Radar, wireless communication, Optical Communication, and more. This marks the 2nd edition of ICVMWT, which will be held at MMMUT Gorakhpur, India.

Proposal: All papers accepted and presented at the conference by duly registered authors will be submitted to the Springer book series.
